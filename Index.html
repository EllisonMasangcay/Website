The Fil-Am orginzation is an orginzaton that mainly do two things. The first formost things that they do is that they help new filipinos new to the area to get adjusted and accustomed to the area.

A Bit Of Everything

A Preview of what we do in as a community throughout the year.

We usually 5 major events during the year, most of them taking place in the end of the year. Two of the event usually takes place in the towards winter and fall. The first major event is the boxing party, where people usually show hours before the actual fight itself. The next event is the Filipino Mass, where a Filipino priest is invited to lead the mass. The next event is is the Easter Party in Nelson's Barn, where a traditional egg hunt takes place, and often a banquet afterwards. The next event is the St. Mary's Fan Dinner, which doubles as fund raisers for countries such as Haiti, and the Philippines itself. The final event of the year is Annual Christmas Party which takes place in December.

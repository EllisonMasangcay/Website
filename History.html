West Tennessee Fil-Am Community

The History of The Community

How it started and why it was created.

It started by a group of young ladies, led by Helen, in the year 1998 as a private social club for a then small group of Filipinos in Jackson, Tennessee in order to organize and hold social events such as Christmas parties and summer picnics.

Overview

The Community is always welcoming new members, and always treat them as family memebers. All are welcome, and all are accepted.
